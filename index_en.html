About Me

Aihua Zhang is an Associate Chief Physician and Master's Supervisor at The Third People's Hospital, Fujian University of Chinese Medicine. She received her Bachelor's degree in Medical Imaging from Fujian Medical University in 2011, and her Master's degree in Medical Imaging and Nuclear Medicine from Fujian Medical University in 2020.

Expertise

Expertise in ultrasound diagnosis of superficial organs such as heart, peripheral blood vessels, thyroid, breast, and musculoskeletal system, as well as transabdominal and transcavitary ultrasound diagnosis of gynecological diseases.
